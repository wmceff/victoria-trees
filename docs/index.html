The Trees of Victoria

About Victoria Trees

The City of Victoria plants and maintains many diverse trees from around the world - this helps you identify and learn a bit about them.

The tree data is mainly from the City of Victoria's trees dataset - we've done some cleanup and made some additions. If your tree is missing, it could be because it's not on public property, or that the city isn't keeping track of it. Check out iNaturalist or PictureThis to help with identification.

Inspired by James Clowater's website Trees of Victoria. We've cleaned up the City's data and provided some additional points of interest about the trees.
